Marriage and Ascension

Velu Nachiyar married Muthu Vaduganatha Periyavudaya Thevar, the ruler of the Sivagangai kingdom, at the age of sixteen. The marriage united two important royal houses and marked the beginning of her role as a queen consort. For several years, she shared power and responsibilities with her husband, supporting him in governing the kingdom and maintaining peace. However, the political landscape of the time was volatile, with the British East India Company aggressively expanding its influence across India.
